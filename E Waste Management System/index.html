About Us

Welcome to E-Kabadi, your one-stop digital solution for efficient and eco-friendly waste management! We are committed to transforming the way households and businesses dispose of recyclable waste by connecting them with trusted kabadiwalas (scrap dealers) through a seamless online platform. E Kabadi is a platform where you can sell your e-waste and contribute to a cleaner environment.

Our Mission

At E-Kabadi, we believe in "Waste to Wealth." Our goal is to promote recycling, reduce landfill waste, and empower users to earn cash from their scrap while contributing to a cleaner, greener planet.
